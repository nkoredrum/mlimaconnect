Terms and Conditions
1. Agent Responsibilities

As a Mlima Connect agent, you will be responsible for assisting farmers in your community to access our platform, providing basic support, and facilitating transactions as needed.

2. Commissions

Agents earn a commission on transactions processed through their account. Commission rates vary by service and will be clearly communicated during the onboarding process.

3. Code of Conduct

Agents are expected to maintain the highest standards of professionalism and integrity. Any fraudulent activity will result in immediate termination and potential legal action.

4. Training

All agents must complete the Mlima Connect training program before they can start operating. Additional training may be required for new services.

5. Data Privacy

Agents must respect user privacy and handle all personal information in accordance with our privacy policy and applicable laws.
